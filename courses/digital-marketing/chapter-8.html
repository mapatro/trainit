Every chapter has the same rhythm: a tight idea → a ● Do this → an AI prompt you paste into Claude/ChatGPT/Gemini to produce a real ClearEaty asset → a ✓ Check yourself. Do them in order — each builds the growth engine.

1

The hero format

The winning structure is short: a hook (a surprising snack), the scan, the score reveal, and a one-line CTA. Under 20 seconds, vertical, captioned.

2

Hooks & scripts

ai prompt · hooks + scripts
› Write 5 short-form video scripts (Reels/Shorts/TikTok) for ClearEaty in the format Hook → Scan → Score reveal → CTA, each ≤20 seconds. Theme: “scan a popular snack, see the honest 1–10 score, find a better pick.” Give a scroll-stopping hook line, on-screen text, voiceover, and a CTA (“Scan yours free — link in bio”). Vary the snacks (incl. some South-Asian-diaspora favourites). No medical claims; honest about whatever score appears.
3

Storyboard & shoot

● Do this
  1. Pick 3 scripts; list the exact products and shots (close-up of label, the scan, the score on screen).
  2. Shoot vertical on a phone in good light; keep it real — authenticity outperforms polish here.
  3. Add big captions (most watch muted) and a clear end card.
ai prompt · captions + thumbnails
› For these 3 ClearEaty video scripts [paste], write: a punchy on-screen caption set, 5 hashtag options per platform (Instagram, YouTube Shorts), and 3 thumbnail/cover text ideas each. Keep the voice honest and non-medical.
✓ Check yourself
  • 5 scripts written, 3 storyboarded
  • Each has a 0–3s hook and a clear CTA
  • Captioned for muted viewing

More: Instagram Creators — Reels best practices

Show the real app and real scores. Don’t stage a score the app wouldn’t give. Remember: ClearEaty is an information tool, not medical advice. Never write “cures,” “treats,” “detox,” or “guaranteed weight loss.” Keep it about knowing what’s in your food.
